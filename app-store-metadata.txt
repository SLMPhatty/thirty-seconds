DESCRIPTION:

thirty is a meditation app that asks for just 30 seconds of your day.

No courses. No subscriptions. No 10-step programs. Just one breath cycle, one moment of stillness, and you're done.

Open the app. Tap begin. Follow the breathing circle. That's it.

Features:
- Guided breathing animation that expands and contracts with your breath
- Ambient sounds: rain, white noise, and singing bowl
- Breathing patterns (box, 4-7-8, coherence) for longer sessions
- Daily streak tracking to build a consistent practice
- Haptic feedback on each inhale
- Dark minimalist interface designed for calm

Try 3 free sessions. Unlock longer durations and premium sounds with a single $4.99 purchase — no subscriptions, ever.

Sometimes thirty seconds is all you need.
