Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 10, 2026

thirty ("the App") is a meditation timer app. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

thirty stores all data locally on your device. This includes:

  • Your meditation streak and session count
  • Your preferences (sound settings, timer duration)
  • Your purchase status (unlocked or free tier)

This data never leaves your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

Analytics

thirty does not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising frameworks. We do not track your usage, location, or behavior.

Purchases

In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information, Apple ID, or billing details.

Third-Party Services

thirty does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or SDKs that collect user data.

Children's Privacy

thirty does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app contains no user accounts, social features, or data collection mechanisms.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
